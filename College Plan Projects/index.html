College

College is an important step in a high schooler's life. During students' senior year, they spend time applying to the colleges they like. However, not every student will be selected by their dream college. It is quite competitive. Knowing how to top the competition is very important. There are many steps to the application process such as letters of recommendation and essays.


There is a right college for everyone
